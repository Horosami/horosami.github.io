01. Chat

Clinet-Server Chat

We all love to chat with friends in the chat. Chat allows you to exchange instant messages. As a demonstration on the image, both the client and the server are logged.

If the user sent a message, then his index is displayed, which allows you to distinguish it among others. Otherwise, you can print the name, as you can see in standard chat rooms.

02. Clicker

Monster Clicker

This clicker allows you to kill monsters. In the game there is a random appearance of monsters and the more the player killed monsters, the more the random appearance of strong ones.

For finishing monsters, gold is given, with which you can improve weapons. Sometimes rubies fall out, with the help of which you can buy assistants.

At the end of a specific time, the best result per game is given. Good luck!

03. Adventure

Interplanetary RPG

It is a journey through different planets and worlds. Here you will admire the beauty and peyzdazha of cosmic nature. You can control the spacecraft, meet unidentified robots, travel through portals and much more. The main task is to find the treasure and save the galaxy. To do this, you must destroy the Guard Zone on the Red Planet. To not die, you can use abilities or find a bottle of potion.

04. CutScene

Combat Lesson CutScene

СutScene assumes movement of the camera and creation of a scene with animators of characters or objects. In this scene, you can observe teenagers who are trained in martial arts. On the background is added the singing of birds and a pleasant melody. Characters were created in the program Adobe Fuse CC. Animations for characters imported from the site Mixamo.
